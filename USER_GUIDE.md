## 4. Marketplace & Credits

LeadForge operates on a credit system for premium lead access.

### Buying Credits
Go to the **Marketplace** tab to purchase packages:
1.  **Starter Pack** ($29): 100 Credits.
2.  **Pro Bundle** ($99): 500 Credits + Pro Tier Upgrade.
3.  **Enterprise Scale** ($299): 2000 Credits + Enterprise Tier Upgrade.

### Transaction History
View your complete purchase history and credit usage log at the bottom of the Marketplace page.

## 5. Mobile App (PWA)

LeadForge is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, meaning you can install it on your phone for a native app experience.

### Installation
1.  **iOS (Safari)**:
    *   Tap the **Share** button.
    *   Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
2.  **Android (Chrome)**:
    *   Tap the menu (three dots).
    *   Tap **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.

### Offline Mode
The app works offline! You can view previously loaded leads and navigate the UI even without an internet connection.
